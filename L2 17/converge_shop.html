Singal 2.0

The Signal Sensor uses Bluetooth to transmit data to the Converge app on your smart device or to the Signal Live Hub which transmits data to the cloud automatically. No wires, no more awkward trailing thermocouples or routes for water ingress.

Signal 2.1

New and more reliable Signal V2.1 version. The latest iteration of our fully wireless embeddable sensor that measures in-situ concrete temperature and compressive strength. It uploads it all to the cloud in real-time via the Signal Live Hub.

Bluetooth to cellular gateway currently being developed by Converge, to transmit data from our BLE sensors, or other beacons, to our servers via cellular.
